The Alienware 17 is a behemoth of a computer. It has an absolutely STUNNING 4K display, lots of ports and surprisingly good battery life for such a power- hungry device. If you are a gamer on-the-go, this is the laptop for you.

  • Dell Inspiron 7559

For the price, this Dell Inspiron 7559 has solid gaming performance, a nice 1080p display, 1TB hard drive and a backlit key board. This laptop is for gamers looking for a solid, affordable, powerful laptop.

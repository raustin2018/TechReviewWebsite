  • Original iPhone

The original iPhone was a feat of engineering. It completely transformed the way we thought of smart phones. After the release of this ground- breaking product, the technology industry was changed forever.

  • iPhone 3G

The iPhone 3G had some major improvements. It added the ability to use 3G technology which greatly improved the data connection speeds that were acheiveable on the device.

  • iPhone 3GS

The iPhone 3GS was a relatively small step beyond the iPhone 3G, but it was still very important. It brought features such as voice control which used the user's voice to do simple tasks such as play music, tell you the time and other small tasks. This was the precursor to Siri and other voice-operated services like Cortana and Google Now.

  • iPhone 4

The iPhone 4 brought a new form-factor to the market. With premium materials and a solid build, nothing like this product had ever really been seen before. Today, we here at Tech Review, think that this was one of the best-made phones in the history of smart phones. The solid squared off edges gave the phone a great feel in the user's hand and the glass back and front of the phone gave it a very sexy look.

  • iPhone 4S

The iPhone 4S was groundbreaking to say the least. It brought new features such as Siri, that let the user have conversations with the device. This was abosolutely astounding. People could ask Siri questions such as, "How are the Padres doing?" or, "What is the weather in San Fransico?". The 4S also brought 4G connectivity which was incredibly fast, to say the least.

  • iPhone 5

The iPhone 5 was a shock to the world. The iPhone 5 had a completely different form-factor than the preceeding iPhone 4 model. With improved LTE connectivity, people were able to connect faster than ever before.

  • iPhone 5C

With the shocking release of the iPhone 5C the world was changed. This was the beginning of the budget smartphone market, and it provided the same features in a similar form factor at a reduced cost. With the release of the iPhone 5C, a new market was created that we see populated today by products such as the 1+3 and the Motorola Moto G.

  • iPhone 5S

The iPhone 5S was astonishing. With the iPhone 5S, we saw some of the world's first consumer biometric scanning. With the finger print sensor fondly known as touch id.

  • iPhone 6

The iPhone 6 changed the widely accepted 4 inch form factor accepted by consumers and manufacturers alike. The phone had many new features including:

  1. Wifi Calling
  2. Optical Image Stabilization
  3. 1080p 60fps video recording
  4. Apple Pay

These features were stunning.

  • iPhone 6S

The iPhone 6s brought many new features. It had live photos which allowed the user to play a quick loop of what was going on when the picture was taken. It also had force-touch which allowed the phone's display to sense pressure from the users finger.

  • iPhone 7 and 7 Plus

The latest iPhone 7 brought major speed upgrades with the new A10 Fusion quad core cpu and the cameras had major upgrades. The iPhone 7+ has 2 cameras which allows for optical zooming and higher definition photos.
